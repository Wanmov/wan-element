Button 按钮 ​

常用的操作按钮。

基础用法 ​

使用 type、plain、 round和 circle来定义按钮的样式。

<template>
    <p>
      <wan-button>Default</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="primary">Primary</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="success">Success</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="info">Info</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="warning">Warning</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="danger">Danger</wan-button>
    </p>
  
    <p>
      <wan-button plain>Plain</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="primary" plain>Primary</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="success" plain>Success</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="info" plain>Info</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="warning" plain>Warning</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="danger" plain>Danger</wan-button>
    </p>
  
    <p>
      <wan-button round>Round</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="primary" round>Primary</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="success" round>Success</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="info" round>Info</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="warning" round>Warning</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="danger" round>Danger</wan-button>
    </p>
  
    <p>
      <wan-button icon="search" circle />
      <wan-button type="primary" icon="edit" circle />
      <wan-button type="success" icon="check" circle />
      <wan-button type="info" icon="message" circle />
      <wan-button type="warning" icon="star" circle />
      <wan-button type="danger" icon="trash" circle />
    </p>
  </template>

禁用状态 ​

你可以使用 disabled 属性来定义按钮是否被禁用。

<template>
    <p>
      <wan-button>Default</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="primary">Primary</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="success">Success</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="info">Info</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="warning">Warning</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="danger">Danger</wan-button>
    </p>
    <p>
      <wan-button disabled>Default</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="primary" disabled>Primary</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="success" disabled>Success</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="info" disabled>Info</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="warning" disabled>Warning</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="danger" disabled>Danger</wan-button>
    </p>
  </template>

图标按钮 ​

使用 icon属性来定义按钮的图标。

<template>
    <div>
      <wan-button type="primary" icon="edit" />
      <wan-button type="primary" icon="share" />
      <wan-button type="primary" icon="trash" />
      <wan-button type="primary" icon="search">Search</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="primary">
        Upload<wan-icon icon="upload" style="margin-left: 8px" />
      </wan-button>
    </div>
  </template>

按钮组 ​

使用 <er-button-group> 对多个按钮分组。

<template>
    <p>
      <wan-button-group disabled>
        <wan-button type="primary" icon="arrow-left">Previous Page</wan-button>
        <wan-button type="primary">
          Next Page<wan-icon icon="arrow-right" style="margin-left: 8px" />
        </wan-button>
      </wan-button-group>
    </p>
    <p>
      <wan-button-group type="warning" size="small">
        <wan-button type="primary" icon="edit" round />
        <wan-button type="primary" icon="share" />
        <wan-button type="primary" icon="trash" round />
      </wan-button-group>
    </p>
  </template>

加载状态 ​

使用 loading属性来定义按钮的加载状态。

TIP

您可以使用 loading 插槽或 loadingIcon 属性自定义您的 loading 图标

ps: loading 插槽优先级高于 loadingIcon 属性

<template>
    <div>
      <wan-button type="primary" loading>Loading</wan-button>
      <wan-button type="primary" loading-icon="circle-notch" loading
        >Loading</wan-button
      >
      <wan-button type="primary" loading>
        <template #loading>
          <wan-icon style="margin-right: 6px" icon="star" spin />
        </template>
        Loading
      </wan-button>
    </div>
  </template>

按钮尺寸 ​

使用 size属性来定义按钮的尺寸。

<template>
    <p>
      <wan-button size="large">Large</wan-button>
      <wan-button>Default</wan-button>
      <wan-button size="small">Small</wan-button>
      <wan-button size="large" icon="search">Search</wan-button>
      <wan-button icon="search">Search</wan-button>
      <wan-button size="small" icon="search">Search</wan-button>
    </p>
    <p>
      <wan-button size="large" round>Large</wan-button>
      <wan-button round>Default</wan-button>
      <wan-button size="small" round>Small</wan-button>
      <wan-button size="large" icon="search" round>Search</wan-button>
      <wan-button icon="search" round>Search</wan-button>
      <wan-button size="small" icon="search" round>Search</wan-button>
    </p>
    <p>
      <wan-button size="large" icon="search" circle />
      <wan-button icon="search" circle />
      <wan-button size="small" icon="search" circle />
    </p>
  </template>

Tag ​

可以自定义元素标签。例如，按钮，div，路由链接，nuxt 链接。

<template>
    <wan-button>button</wan-button>
    <wan-button tag="div" role="button" tabindex="0">div</wan-button>
    <wan-button
      type="primary"
      tag="a"
      href="https://github.com/Wanmov/wan-element"
      target="_blank"
      rel="noopener noreferrer"
    >
      a
    </wan-button>
  </template>

节流模式 ​

可以通过 useThrottle 属性来定义按钮是否使用节流模式 默认为 true。

<script setup></script>

<template>
  <p>
    <wan-button @click="handleBtnClick"> with throttle</wan-button>
    <wan-button :use-throttle="false" @click="handleBtnClick"
      >without throttle</wan-button
    >
  </p>
</template>
